Who Are We?

We are The Big Country Backpackers! Were a small backpacking company that is based out of Sacramento, California. We focus on the quality of products rather than the dollars in our pockets. The worst thing in the world is to have your gear fail you while out in the country. Thats why we hand pick through products and only bring you the best so that you can spend less time with your gear and more time on your adventure!

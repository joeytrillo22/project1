What does Big Country Certified mean?

Big Country Certified means that this product has been tested and approved by us here at Big Country Backpackers. When you purchase something from our Big Country store you know that you are getting a quality product that has been tested to its limits! We don't want our products failing you out in the country so every product must get our stamp of approval before being sold to our Big Country Nation.
